    Types of deposits

    Fixed-term: deposits with or without the right of early withdrawal

    Deposit line: client can replenish and withdraw funds within minimum and maximum limits

    Bank deposit framework agreement: a framework agreement of deposit account, under which client can deposit funds in tranches for different terms

    Interest payment

    2. Compounding interest
    - yearly
    - upon maturity
